# Clearwater Services Reference

Use these exact service names when identifying products in scope for a deal. This is a static reference — update only when Clearwater's service offerings change.

---

## 1. Cybersecurity Program Leadership & Transformation

- **vCISO / Program Leadership** (vCISO, vCPO, vCCO) — Experienced healthcare cybersecurity/compliance leaders to design and run a standards-based program. Use when client lacks senior leadership capacity.
- **Strategic & Tactical Planning** — Program planning aligned to frameworks (NIST CSF) and client priorities. Use when client needs a sequenced initiative plan.
- **Training & Workforce Enablement** — Workforce training and program enablement. Use when client has skill gaps, turnover, or inconsistent practices.

## 2. Risk Analysis & Risk Management

- **HIPAA Risk Analysis (OCR-aligned)** — Healthcare-focused risk analysis aligned to HIPAA Security Rule and OCR guidance. Use when client needs a defensible baseline or has enforcement/insurance pressure.
- **Risk Management Planning & Execution Support** — Action plans and risk response support including implementation management. Use when client has identified risks but lacks remediation capacity.
- **Maturity Assessment & Trending** — Assessment against NIST CSF with trending over time. Use when client needs to measure progress or justify investment to leadership.

## 3. Technical Testing & Engineering

- **Vulnerability Scanning (Internal/External)** — Continuous or periodic identification and prioritization of vulnerabilities.
- **Penetration Testing (Internal/External/Wireless)** — Authorized simulated attacks to test safeguards and exploitability.
- **Web & Mobile Application Penetration Testing** — Application-layer testing for misconfigurations and weaknesses.
- **Phishing / Social Engineering Testing** — Simulated social engineering to assess awareness and human-factor risk.
- **Security Engineering & Remediation Support** — Implementation support for technical controls (config guidance, tool eval, staff augmentation).
- **Cloud Security (Assessment & Design)** — Assessment and architecture guidance for securing cloud environments.
- **Network Assessment & Design** — Network security assessment against best practices with improvement recommendations.
- **Security Controls Validation (SCVA)** — Validation of how controls respond to realistic attack scenarios.
- **Medical Device & IoT Security** — Device discovery, vulnerability assessment, and device security program support.

## 4. Resiliency & Incident Readiness

- **Business Impact Analysis (BIA)** — Identifies critical processes, RTO/RPO requirements, and recovery prioritization.
- **Incident Response Program Assessment** — Review of IR plans, playbooks, policies and procedures.
- **Ransomware Readiness Review** — Focused assessment of ransomware preparedness.
- **Incident Response Plan / Playbook Development** — Development or update of IR plan and playbooks.
- **Incident Response Tabletop Exercise** — Standard or immersive tabletop to test decision-making.

## 5. Managed Security Services & Managed Cloud

- **Managed Detection & Response (MDR)** — 24x7x365 SOC monitoring, threat hunting, containment and response using EDR and healthcare intel.
- **Log Management / SIEM** — Centralized log collection, correlation, retention and investigation support.
- **Managed Vulnerability Management** — Continuous scanning, prioritization and reporting for attack surface reduction.
- **Firewall Management** — Configuration, monitoring, policy management and operational support.
- **Managed Azure Cloud Services** — Design/implementation plus 24x7 operations for Azure environments.

## 6. Privacy, Compliance, Audit & Certification

- **HIPAA Security Rule Assessment** — Assessment against HIPAA Security Rule standards.
- **HIPAA Privacy / Breach Notification Assessments** — Assessments for Privacy and Breach Notification requirements.
- **Information Blocking Assessment** — Assessment for information blocking compliance.
- **Audit & Certification Readiness (SOC 2, HITRUST, PCI, CMMC)** — Readiness assessments and preparation support.
- **IT Audit Services** — Cybersecurity audits aligned to specific frameworks.

## 7. Managed Cybersecurity & Compliance Programs

- **ClearAdvantage Managed Program** — Multi-year managed program to design, implement, and operate a security and HIPAA compliance program. For regional/critical access orgs seeking outsourced model.
- **ClearConfidence** — Ongoing risk analysis, risk management and compliance for larger, complex environments. Includes IRM|Pro subscription.
- **Cybersecurity Assist Partner Program (CAPP)** — Multi-year advisory, testing, and compliance services as a partner program.
- **Vendor Risk Management as a Service (VRM)** — Assessment and ongoing monitoring of third-party vendor security risk.
- **Patient Privacy Monitoring Services (PPMS)** — Monitoring of user activity in EHR and apps with managed exception handling.

## 8. IRM|Pro Software Platform

- **IRM|Analysis** — Risk analysis module
- **IRM|Security** — Security management module
- **IRM|Privacy** — Privacy management module
- **IRM|405(d) HICP** — Health Industry Cybersecurity Practices module
- **IRM|Performance** — Performance and reporting module
