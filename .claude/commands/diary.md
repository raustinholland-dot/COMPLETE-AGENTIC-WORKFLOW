## Style Guide

- **Tone**: Founder's blog post. Lighthearted, funny, self-deprecating. Not serious/academic.
- **Visual style**: ai-2027.com — light theme, white background, single column, 720px max-width, editorial feel
- **Metrics**: Track paradigm shifts, tools tried, money spent, system scorecard, approach evolution
- **Key themes**: "Humans being middleware," "there's something beautiful about failure," "language is code," the Gemini old lover, the ceiling moments
- **Voice**: First person. Austin's voice — direct, honest, occasionally profane about Microsoft products.
- **Privacy**: No company names, no colleague full names, no deal specifics. Keep it generic enough to share publicly.
