What to Notice (v3a vs v3)

  • Edge contrast is dramatic: Regional Clinic has perfectly smooth, clean arcs. Enterprise Health System has elaborate snowflake-like fractal edges with sub-peak branching. The silhouettes are unmistakably different.
  • Arm width tells the story: Regional Clinic's arms are narrow and delicate. Enterprise Health System's arms are wide and substantial. You can feel the deal's weight.
  • Color blooms outward: Inner rings are muted and desaturated. Outer rings are vivid and rich. Mature deals look like they're blooming with color at their edges.
  • Branches curve with complexity: Regional Clinic's single branch is nearly straight. Enterprise Health System's branches curve organically like roots.
  • Background texture: Simple deals float on clean darkness. Complex deals sit on a faint radial grain, adding visual weight.
