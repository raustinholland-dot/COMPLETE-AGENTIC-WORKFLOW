What to Notice

  • Regional Clinic is sparse: Small center, tiny dimension nodes, very few artifact dots, one stakeholder with one thin connection. Lots of empty space.
  • Acme Health fills out: Larger center, mid-sized dimension nodes with visible artifact clusters, three stakeholders with crossing connection lines.
  • Enterprise Health System is dense: Large glowing center, big green dimension nodes surrounded by dense artifact clouds, eight stakeholders with many crossing bezier curves. The visualization feels full and alive.
  • Voids are visible: Dimensions with low scores have small nodes and sparse artifact children. You can immediately see where evidence is missing.
  • Stakeholder activity: Recently active stakeholders pulse with a glow. Dormant ones fade.
  • Hover to explore: Mouse over any node to highlight its connections and see details.
