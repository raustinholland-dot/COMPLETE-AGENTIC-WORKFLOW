What to Notice

  • Edges tell complexity at a glance: Regional Clinic's mandala has perfectly smooth arcs. Acme Health shows visible serrations. Enterprise Health System has elaborate snowflake-like fractal branching on every arm edge.
  • Branches tell the stakeholder story: Regional Clinic has a single thin dashed branch (one unconfirmed PC). Acme Health grows three branches with mixed styles. Enterprise Health System sprouts eight branches, including thick solid C/ES/DM lines.
  • Size encodes value: The center circle grows from tiny ($75K) to medium ($285K) to large ($1.2M), with proportionally stronger radial glow.
  • Color progression: Arms shift from red/amber (early weak scores) to green (mature strong scores) as rings grow outward through time.
  • Dormant bands: Enterprise Health System shows a visible gap ring from the summer stall (Aug-Sep 2025), preserving temporal honesty.
  • DAP boundary: Absent on Regional Clinic, partial on Acme Health, fully complete on Enterprise Health System.
  • PE Sponsor halo: Invisible on Regional Clinic, growing gold ring on Acme Health, bright full halo on Enterprise Health System.
  • Trend pulse: The outermost ring subtly pulses on improving deals, creating a breathing effect at the mandala's edge.
