What's New in v4

  • Evidence feathers replace solid wedges: Instead of flat color bands, each arm is filled with individual artifact marks. A dimension with 2 emails looks sparse and skeletal. One with 30 artifacts looks dense and full. You instantly see where evidence exists and where voids remain.
  • Stakeholder network ring: Nodes orbit outside the DAP boundary. Connection lines run from each stakeholder inward to the dimensions they've touched. Thick lines = frequent communication. Thin/absent = gap.
  • Activity arcs: Each stakeholder node has a colored arc showing engagement recency. Full bright arc = active this week. Fading partial arc = going dark. No arc = silent for 14+ days.
  • Hover interaction: Hover over any arm to see its dimension detail. Hover a stakeholder node to highlight their connections. The network topology becomes explorable.
  • Bespoke silhouettes: No two deals look alike. Regional Clinic is sparse and airy. Enterprise Health System is dense and elaborate. The shape IS the deal.
